Research Interests

Finite Element modelling

I have been collaborating on research into modelling of continuum problems using the Finite Element Method (FEM) and development of the popular FEniCS software platform. Recent work is in partnership with other institutions through the UKRI ExCALIBUR Programme, and the Coupling, Synthesis and Performance Project and High Priority Use Case SysGenX.
